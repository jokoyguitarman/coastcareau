About Coastcare Vets

At Coastcare, we're animal lovers first, vets second. Founded by Dr Jess, a Sydney university-trained small-animal veterinarian (and a home full of three dogs and four cats), Coastcare was created to make vet care easier, flexible and most importantly, personal.

Between work, school runs, and everyday chaos, getting to the vet isn't always simple — but your pets still deserve the best care. That's why we bring the clinic to you, wherever your pets feel safe and you feel supported. It's expert care, minus the car ride and waiting-room stress.

Our Mission

To make veterinary care accessible, compassionate, and stress-free — connecting pets and their people with trusted care in the comfort of home.

Meet Dr Jess

Your trusted mobile veterinarian

Dr Jess is passionate about helping animals live happier, healthier lives. A 2019 graduate of the University of Sydney, she has special interests in dermatology, small-animal medicine and soft-tissue surgery. Her calm, friendly approach helps pets (and their owners) feel at ease from the moment she arrives.

Her own lively crew of pets inspires her every day — reminding her that vet care isn't just about medicine, it's about empathy, understanding, and connection.
