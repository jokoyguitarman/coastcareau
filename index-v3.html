Our Mobile Veterinary Services

Comprehensive care for cats and dogs, delivered with compassion and expertise

Health Checks & Vaccinations

Comprehensive health examinations and vaccinations for kittens, puppies, and adult pets to keep them healthy and protected.

Flea, Tick & Heartworm Prevention

Annual injections and preventive treatments to protect your pet from parasites and heartworm disease.

Senior Health Checks

Comprehensive health assessments for aging pets, including bloodwork and monitoring for age-related conditions.

Arthritis Injections

Pain relief injections and treatments to help manage arthritis and improve your pet's mobility and comfort.

Skin Consultations

Specialized dermatology consultations to diagnose and treat skin conditions, allergies, and coat problems.

Nail Trimming

Professional nail trimming and grooming services to keep your pet's paws healthy and comfortable.

Anal Gland Expressions

Professional anal gland expression to prevent discomfort and potential health issues for your pet.

Compassionate Home Euthanasia

Gentle, dignified end-of-life care in the comfort of your home, surrounded by family and familiar surroundings.

Meet Dr Jess

Dr Jess is passionate about helping animals live happier, healthier lives. A 2019 graduate of the University of Sydney, she has special interests in dermatology, small-animal medicine and soft-tissue surgery.

Her own lively crew of pets inspires her every day — reminding her that vet care isn't just about medicine, it's about empathy, understanding, and connection.
